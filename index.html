Discover Assam- The Gateway to Northeast India

Experience the land of lush tea gardens

Assam is a beautiful state in northeastern India, renowned for its scenic landscapes, world-famous tea plantations, the majestic Brahmaputra River, and rich cultural heritage. Home to diverse communities, Assam offers a unique blend of history, nature, wildlife, and traditions that attract visitors from around the world.

Why visit ASSAM ?

Festivals Assam is known for its vibrant festivals, with Bihu being the most significant. These celebrations showcase the state's cultural richness through traditional music, dance, and cuisine.
Wildlife Assam is one of India's richest biodiversity hotspots. Dense forests, wetlands, and national parks shelter elephants, tigers, wild buffalo, rare birds, and the iconic one-horned rhinoceros.
Tea Heritage Assam is globally renowned for its tea production. Visitors can tour tea estates, witness the tea-making process, and savor the distinct flavors of Assam tea.
Culture and Traditions Assam is home to diverse ethnic communities, each with its own unique traditions, crafts, and festivals. Visitors can explore local markets, traditional dance forms, and handicrafts that reflect the state's rich cultural heritage.
