Discover Assam- The Gateway to Northeast India

Experience the land of lush tea gardens, mighty rivers, vibrant festivals, rich wildlife, and diverse cultures. Assam welcomes you with breathtaking landscapes and timeless traditions.

Assam is a beautiful state in northeastern India, renowned for its scenic landscapes, world-famous tea plantations, the majestic Brahmaputra River, and rich cultural heritage. Home to diverse communities, Assam offers a unique blend of history, nature, wildlife, and traditions that attract visitors from around the world.

Why visit ASSAM ?

Cultures & Traditions
Assam is home to numerous ethnic communities, each contributing to its vibrant traditions. Music, dance, handloom weaving, and colorful festivals reflect the state's rich cultural identity. Traditional attire like the Mekhela Chador and handcrafted silk from Sualkuchi are symbols of Assamese heritage.
